👑 自然之诗

Nature's Poetry
2024 · 10 幅作品

与自然的对话——森林、湖泊、花园、秋叶、日落。Aria 倾听自然的低语，将其转化为视觉的诗篇。

Dialogues with nature — forests, lakes, gardens, autumn leaves, sunset skies. Aria listens to nature's whispers and translates them into visual poetry.
